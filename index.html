FAQ

What is a raspberry pi ?
A raspberry Pi is a mini Linux based computer, the size of a credit card. It consumes as much power as your iPhone charger, and works great if you require a computer that runs 24 hours, 7 days a week.
But I can build it myself ?
Yeap, you can ! This site is meant for people who do not wish to trouble themselves with learning how to configure a raspverry pi. Or perhaps its just simpler to outsource things so that you can focus on other more important stuff..
